Patrícia Marques

"I'm the kind of person who says hi to dogs."

I'm 22 and I'm from Lisbon, Portugal. Graduated in Visual Arts and Technologies and, at the moment, I am studying for my Master in New Media and Web Practices.

I've always had a special taste for photography, post-production and coding. Initially, it was just for fun, but as I acquired tools and knowledge at a technical and theoretical level, I aroused greater interest in the area.

I like to explore the many faces of a project in search of the best possible result, never settling for just what is acceptable. I love to travel and discover new places.
