INCLUE

An experience for everyone!

INCLUE is a project about digital accessibility developed within the scope of the Master in New Media and Web Practices, together with 3 colleagues.

Our objective is, through interviews with real testimonies, of what it is like to live in the digital age with some disability that makes digital accessibility difficult, to educate and sensitize students and future professionals in the area.

We created several versions of the same video, in which we present a completely inaccessible version to the user. After viewing this, you will be able to choose a "character" and view the video from the point of view of his disability. That said, you will realize the problem in question and have access to excerpts from the interview and images provided by the selected user. On the page about the project, we present the ideal version of this video, accessible to everyone.

The web page of the project is not finished yet, it is just an outline of our idea. However, it is now possible to visit and view all contents. Click here to see it for yourself!
